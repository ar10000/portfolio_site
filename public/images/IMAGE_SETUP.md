## üöÄ Quick Steps

1. **Take screenshots** of your projects
2. **Resize** to the sizes above (use any image editor)
3. **Optimize** at [TinyPNG.com](https://tinypng.com) (free)
4. **Save** in `public/images/projects/` with exact filenames above
5. **Done!** Images will appear automatically

## üí° Tips

- Use [Squoosh.app](https://squoosh.app) for advanced optimization
